Features

Everything you need from an image viewer

Designed for speed and versatility, without the bloat.

Two-pass progressive loading
Images appear instantly with a fast preview, then sharpen to full resolution — no waiting for large files.
Filmstrip with parallel loading
Thumbnail strip loads in the background with prefetch. Supports recursive mode to browse entire folder trees.
Animated GIF & WebP playback
Frame-accurate animation playback with full pan and zoom support — even while playing.
Built-in media player
Play video and audio files directly — MP4, MKV, MP3, FLAC and more. No separate app needed.
RAW & specialist formats
Full RAW decoding for Canon, Nikon, Sony, Fujifilm and more. Plus PSD compositing and FITS auto-stretch for astronomers.
Session restore
Reopens exactly where you left off — folder, image, window size, splitter state, everything.
Always-visible search bar
Filter images in the filmstrip instantly. Matches are highlighted in the thumbnail labels.
Navigator minimap
When zoomed in, a minimap shows your current viewport position over the full image.

Handles virtually any file you throw at it

From everyday JPEGs to specialist scientific and professional formats.
